Summer in Sikkim

Summer in Sikkim aren't as harsh as everywhere else in India. During this season, the weather remains pleasant, thus making it the best time to visit Sikkim for certain parts of the state. During this time, one can also enjoy the flower festival. Summer is warm and moderate and is the ideal season. The average daytime temperature is 5°C to 25°C.
