Winter in Sikkim

The winter season in Sikkim is charming and perfect for vacation as the whole area is covered in snow. Most areas of the state receive Snowfall and get engulfed in the blanket of thick snow, thus luring tourists. During November, even though it doesn't snow, the temperature is quite appropriate for sightseeing. The average daytime temperature is -5°C to 7°C.

Some examples of Sikkim's beauty during Winter

Sikkim Is best know for its snowfall in winter. One can feel the festivity everywhere, on the streets, in the market, even in the monasteries. A great number of festivals are celebrated during the months of winter. Almost all the regions of sikkim are covered with a thick layer of snow. The Snowfall of Sikkim increases the beauty of Sikkim.
