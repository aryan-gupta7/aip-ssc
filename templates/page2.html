Climatic Beauty of Sikkim

Sikkim, which lies in the lap of Himalayas is one of the coldest places in the country. The Climate of Sikkim is very pleasant. There are mainly three seasons are there in Sikkim namely Summer, Winter and Rainy or Monsoon. Winter season in Sikkim usually held between month of January to March. Most areas of the state receive snowfall and get engulfed in the blanket of thick snow, thus luring tourists during the months of Winter.
